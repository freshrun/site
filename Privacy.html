FreshRun Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

FreshRun is designed to help you discover and navigate running routes while respecting your privacy. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app, how it is used, and the third-party services involved in providing the app.

1. Overview

FreshRun is built to minimize the amount of personal data used. Saved routes, activities, and most app preferences are stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated in this policy.

FreshRun does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not use in-app analytics or tracking for advertising purposes.

2. Information We Process

Location Data

FreshRun uses your device location to generate nearby running routes, support route-based features, and provide live navigation during a run.

  • When generating routes, location data may be used to calculate a route near your chosen or current position.
  • When using Live Run, the app may process your precise location and heading continuously to provide turn-by-turn guidance.
  • Background location may be used during an active run so navigation can continue when your screen is locked.

FreshRun stores route and activity records locally on your device. FreshRun does not use your location for advertising or profiling.

Email Login Data

FreshRun uses email-based login to identify users and provide access to the app. When you sign in, you receive a one-time verification code sent to your email address.

Email-based authentication is provided using Auth0. Auth0 processes authentication-related information, such as your email address and login activity, in order to verify your identity and provide sign-in services to FreshRun.

FreshRun may retain your email address as part of your user identity in order to provide access to the app and related services.

FreshRun may also use your email address to contact you occasionally for product-related purposes, such as requesting feedback or suggestions to help improve the app. These communications are infrequent and focused solely on improving FreshRun.

We may retain a minimal record of your email address where necessary for security, fraud prevention, or to ensure we do not contact you again if you request not to be contacted.

We may also retain emails you send to us for support or feedback purposes.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Routing and Geocoding Requests

FreshRun uses its own routing infrastructure together with OpenRouteService and open map data to generate routes and geocoding results.

When you generate a route, search for a location, or use reverse geocoding, request data such as coordinates or search text may be sent through FreshRun’s routing infrastructure and to service providers involved in returning a routing or geocoding result.

FreshRun is designed so that route and geocoding requests are used to provide the requested result and are not used for advertising or user profiling by FreshRun.

Connectivity and Security Data

When the app communicates with FreshRun services, network and security providers such as Cloudflare may process technical information like IP address, request metadata, and device or browser characteristics for security, abuse prevention, and service delivery purposes.

FreshRun may also send basic service checks, such as server health or availability checks, to confirm that routing services are online and working properly.

3. Data Storage

Saved routes, activities, disclaimer acceptance, appearance settings, and similar app preferences are stored locally on your device.

If you use iCloud Backup or other Apple backup features, local app data may be included in your encrypted device backup managed by Apple.

You can remove local app data from your device using the Clear All Data option in the app.

4. How We Use Information

FreshRun uses information only as needed to operate core app features, including:

  • Generating running routes.
  • Providing live navigation and voice guidance.
  • Letting users sign in securely with email verification codes.
  • Maintaining app reliability, routing availability, and service security.

5. Third-Party Services

FreshRun relies on third-party services to provide mapping, authentication, routing, and network protection features. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Auth0 – email login and authentication services.
  • Apple Maps / MapKit – map display, system location features, and certain search/location functionality.
  • OpenRouteService (HeiGIT) / OpenStreetMap data – route generation and certain geocoding or reverse geocoding features.
  • Cloudflare – network security, performance, and request delivery.

Please review their privacy information for more detail:

  • Auth0 Privacy Policy
  • Apple Maps Privacy Policy
  • HeiGIT / OpenRouteService Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can log out of the app from within FreshRun.
  • You can use Clear All Data to remove locally stored app data and reset the app on your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

FreshRun is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly design the app to collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product, legal, or service changes. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.
