Last updated: October 2025

FreshRun is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app and its connected services collect, how it is used, and how it is kept secure.

1. Information We Collect

FreshRun itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app only processes information necessary to provide its core functionality which is generating running routes and offering real-time navigation. This processing happens primarily on your device.

Routing Requests

When you request a route, the selected start and end points are sent securely to OpenRouteService (an open-source routing provider) to calculate the path.

FreshRun does not store or log the route request, start point, or destination.

OpenRouteService may temporarily process routing data to calculate your selected path, in accordance with their own privacy policy.

This data is automatically deleted after a short period and is never used by FreshRun for analytics or tracking.

2. Data Storage

All route and activity data you create in FreshRun is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to or stored in the cloud. If you uninstall the app or reset your device, that data is deleted.

If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your FreshRun data (such as saved routes or runs) may be included in your encrypted device backup managed by Apple.
