FreshRun Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

FreshRun is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app and its connected services collect, how it is used, and how it is kept secure.

1. Information We Collect

FreshRun itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app uses the following information:

Location Data:

This information stays on your device and is never uploaded, shared, or stored on external servers by the app. Apple will ask for your permission before location access is enabled.

Network Data (via Cloudflare):
When the app communicates with the FreshRun routing server, Cloudflare may temporarily log your IP address and basic device information (such as browser or OS type) for security and performance purposes. This is standard for all Cloudflare-protected services and is used only to maintain network reliability and prevent abuse.

2. Data Storage

All route and activity data you create in FreshRun is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to or stored in the cloud. If you uninstall the app or reset your device, that data is deleted.

If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your FreshRun data (such as saved routes or runs) may be included in your encrypted device backup managed by Apple.
